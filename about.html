About Us

Established in 1964, Panchal Castings is a family-owned and operated steel foundry located in Vapi, Gujarat, India. For over five decades, we have built a strong reputation for delivering quality steel castings through the time-tested hand molding process.

Our clients range from manufacturers in the valve and pump industry to companies involved in general engineering. We take pride in our legacy of precision, craftsmanship, and unwavering customer trust passed down through generations.

With an experienced team and traditional expertise, we produce reliable castings tailored to our customers' needs. At Panchal Castings, consistency, integrity, and quality are at the heart of everything we do.
